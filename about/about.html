WHO AM I

Hello, I am a native speaker of Brazilian Portuguese who was born and raised in Ceará, a state in the northeast of the country. Passionate about reading, I grew up surrounded by books on philosophy, science, history, and with a lot of influence from cultures of different countries, a factor that contributed significantly to my education and to the choice of the translation profession.

Enthusiast of language studies. Currently, with a degree in Linguistics/Translation in progress, an opportunity that made me develop the necessary skills to provide a high-quality translation. Ensuring style and context in the target language while applying proper parameters such as grammar, syntax, semantics and appropriate terminology. I have worked with translation from English to Portuguese for 4 years, helping agencies and clients to reach an international audience and share their content in the digital world.
